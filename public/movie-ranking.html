The Best Movies According to Kayla

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time:

1. The Grinch

One of my favourite Christmas Movies is the Grinch, specifically Jim Carrey's verion. It's not Christmas until I've watched this movie!

2. Secondhand Lions

This is such a heartwarming movie to me. I love the storyline of the uncle's time overseas, but my favourite part has to be Walter's relationship with Jasmine the lion.

3. The Office

I understand the Office is a show, not a movie. However, the humor in this show deserves appreciation in my list because it's remained a go-to in my life on a daily basis.
